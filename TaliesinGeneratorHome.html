The Taliesin D&D Character Generator

by Paul Taliesin (2021)

'Taliesin D&D' is a homebrew version of D&D for 'classic' or old school play. This webpage allows you to create an unlimited number of unique and colourful 1st-level characters.

If you'd rather just get to it, and you know how it works, here is a "clean" version, instead:

Once you have created your character, give them a name and then save the page, print, copy onto paper by hand (you'll need to modify it as you play, after all!), or take a screenshot. Reloading the page will generate a new character - the data does not get saved. As a final step, before you start play, look over your equipment and feel free to cross out anything that's unnecessary or that's weighing you down. Good luck and happy adventuring!
